Troubleshooting for Download Issues

How to Import Extensions into Your Browser

This section explains how to import extensions into your browser. These instructions are for Google Chrome, but you can also find guides for other browsers below.

  1. First, download the ZIP file by clicking the "Download" button above.
  2. Once the ZIP file is downloaded, extract the folder to a location on your computer.
  3. After extracting, locate the extracted folder (it will contain extension files, including a `.html`, `.js`, or similar file).
  4. Next, open your Chrome browser and go to the Extensions page (chrome://extensions/).
  5. Enable "Developer mode" at the top-right, then click "Load unpacked" and select the folder where the extracted extension files are located.
